Company Overview

SN Emerson Investment Ltd (RC No: 1844163) specializes in commodity trading, logistics, procurement, and support services across global and African markets.

Commodity Trading

We trade agro-allied products, minerals, metals, renewable energy feedstocks, frozen foods, canned foods, spices, petroleum products, chemicals, and precious metals including lithium.

Support Services

We assist companies entering African markets with due diligence, market research, strategic sales, and procurement services.

Logistics Services

We provide haulage, freight forwarding, clearing, handling of import/export, and complete supply chain management.

Packaging

We process and package a variety of raw materials for different industrial applications.
